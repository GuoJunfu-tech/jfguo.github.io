About Me

Welcome to my personal website! I'm a Ph.d student at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I am also an Ph.D researcher at Graphics&Geometric Computing Laboratory School of Mathematical Sciences, advised by Prof. Liu Ligang at USTC and Prof. Hu Ruizhen at Shenzhen University, and I work closely with Li Changhao (Ph.D student), Cao Hezhi (Ph.D student) here.

My major is Computational Mathematics and I'm passionate about computer graphics, robotics. My research interest lies in combining artificial intelligence and computer graphics, robotics together to solve real-world problems. I'm especially interested in multi-agent collaboration systems, reinforcement learning, and computer vision. My research objective is to develop intelligent systems that can improve the robots' abilities, to pull a better cyber future closer.
